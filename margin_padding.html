Margin and Padding.

=>It is to show Padding(inner-spacing).
=>Meaning to leave space on the left, right, top and bottom.
=>It is done by using Padding.
=>Remember the height will increase as the there will be extra padding space.
=>So, remember to adjust the height and width of the box as to not increase its height and width more than of its parents.
=>Padding: top&bottom left&right;(if you give two values, the first value will be the space for the top and bottom and the second value will be the left and right space.).
=>It is to show Margin(outer-spacing.)
=>box-sizing:content-box;(by default)and we change it to
box-sizing:border-box;(It will adjust the box height and width.) =>It is applicable only for padding and border.
=>>It automatically adjust the height and width of the box according to its parents.
